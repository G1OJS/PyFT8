Document in progress to show details of creating an FT8 signal, adding it to an existing spectrum with other FT8 signals, and decoding the resulting merged spectrum to recover the original signal. The added signal is 'VK1ABC VK3JPK QF22' and can be seen in the outlined pattern at about 870 Hz.
